About

I’m an ambitious digital product designer with a passion for creating simple and elegant solutions to complex problems. As an advocate of open, inclusive design, I believe that the best design is that which works for everyone.

For more than 8 years, I have helped develop industry leading digital services for companies across a variety of sectors.

When I’m not working, I enjoy long walks with my dog, playing football, going to the cinema and relaxing with a good book.

If you’d like to talk about how I could help your company, please don’t hesitate to get in touch: hello@greatscott.design.
